ATAT (Alert Today. Alive Tomorrow.) is a blog by a socially responsible students of College of Immaculate Conception, Cabanatuan City, Nueva Ejica, designed to show how it is absolutely possible to save one's life despite of the natural catastrophe in global. This blog contains knowledge about the different natural disaster like Volcanic Eruption, Earthquake and Tsunami, and also it repose awareness before, during and after the natural disasters.
